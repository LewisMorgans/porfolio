Quote Engine Re-Write
2019 - 2020

My team was created with the sole purpose of completely re-writing the Admiral quotation engine. Bringing Admiral inline with current technology by removing an outdated custom angular.js framework, and implementing Angular 8. Making use of many technologies such as NGRX state management, the RXJS library, allowing us to programme reactively leveraging Observables, composing asynchronous and callback code. This inline with Java-based API's on a maven middle-tier meant we were able to deliver a significantly faster, more powerful, adaptable and scalable solution to serve Admiral products for years to come. Implementing this new engine reduced loading times, error reports and increased customer retention. This was a large project that took several hundreds of hours to complete. To do this we needed to streamline our work process. One example of this was removing the native Angular testbed and creating custom unit tests running in 3.5 seconds opposed 15 minutes. Heavy planning, use of current Software paradigms and following the development lifecycle was the reason that enabled us to deliver a highly functioning product in little time.
