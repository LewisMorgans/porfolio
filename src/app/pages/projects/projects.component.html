Quote Engine Re-Write
2019 - 2020

My team was created with the sole purpose of completely rewriting the Admiral quotation engine. The objective was to bring Admiral in line with current technology by removing an outdated Angular.js framework and implementing Angular 8, making use of many technologies such as NGRX state management and the RXJS library (allowing us to program reactively leveraging observables to compose asynchronous and callback code). This, in line with Java-based APIs on a Maven middle-tier, meant we were able to deliver a significantly faster, more powerful, adaptable and scalable solution to serve Admiral products for years to come. Implementing this new engine reduced loading times, error reports and increased customer retention. This was a large project that took several hundreds of hours to complete. To do this we needed to streamline our work process, involving for example removing the native Angular testbed and creating custom unit tests running in 3.5 seconds as opposed to 15 minutes. Heavy planning, use of current software paradigms and following the development lifecycle enabled us to deliver a highly functioning product in little time.
